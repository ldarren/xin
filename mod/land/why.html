Why use Kloud Konsole mail client?

It is the only free S3 mail client.

Secure and private

Kloud Konsole's open source web client connects to your AWS account directly, We do no send or store any data on our backend. Kloud Konsole uses AWS cognito (of your AWS account) to secure the connections to AWS

Easy to setup

No coding or cloud computing knowledge required, all you need is setup an accout with Kloud Konsole and tell Kloud Konsole how to connect to your AWS account.

No more mail forwarding nightmare

mail forwarding is a common practice to read AWS SES mail on your existng mail client such as Gmail. This option comes with compromises of mandatory mail content manipulation/modification, no central user access control to your mails, silence failure due to reason such as mail size over 10mb, etc
