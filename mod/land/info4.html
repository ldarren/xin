AWS Pricing

While KloudKonsole webmail client is absolutely free to use, Your emails are actually delivered by AWS and AWS services are not free, check the pricing here:

  • SES charges by message and chunk size, see SES pricing page
  • S3 charges by storage size and request count, see S3 pricing page
  • Cognito charges by monthly active user (MAU), see Cognito pricing page

With monthly free 50,000 MAU and 1000 incoming message, the cost will be low compare with other options
